The Relationship between Huawei and Google is becoming more and more complicated

Little by little we are approaching the launch of a new flagship from Huawei. The new P40 Pro is just around the corner, but the loss of Google services could have a very unpleasant impact on the launch. The tumultuous relationship between Huawei and Google kept the top of the list of all blacklist news. This prompted the Chinese tech giant to develop its own such services and so Huawei Mobile Services was born. A suite of applications with a complete app store and even features such as navigation. One thing that was missing from this suite is a search engine. I used the past tense at the beginning of the paragraph because the lack of a search engine is the domain of the past. Huawei has launched a search service that will be equipped by default on new devices. In this way, the Japanese company manages to create a completely new eco-system, based on Android.
